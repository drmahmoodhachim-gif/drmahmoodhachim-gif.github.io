Brief Description to Include

Write 2–3 sentences describing:

  • Your name and project (e.g., Mutho – Epilepsy)
  • How many significant DEGs you found
  • One or two genes or pathways that stand out to you (and why, in the context of your project)

Example: "I am Mutho, working on epilepsy. I found 10 significant DEGs. GENE_001 and GENE_002 were strongly upregulated, which could relate to ion channel dysfunction in seizure disorders."
